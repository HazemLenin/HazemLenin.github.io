# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Two overlapping audiences: recruiters/hiring managers scanning for a mid-senior full-stack (.NET/Angular) hire, and prospective freelance clients evaluating Hazem for project work. Both are time-pressed and skimming — they need to size up skill level, experience, and credibility fast, then find a way to reach out (resume, contact, portfolio proof).

## Product Purpose
A single-page portfolio site (Astro + Tailwind v4) that establishes Hazem Lenin as a sharp, credible, modern full-stack engineer. Success = a visitor forms a strong impression within seconds and takes one of: download resume, view project work, or make contact.

## Brand Personality
Sharp, credible, modern. Confident technical voice — no filler, no false modesty, no generic "passionate developer" language. Precision over decoration.

## Anti-references
Generic AI-template look: cream/sand backgrounds, gradient text, tiny uppercase eyebrows above every section, numbered 01/02/03 scaffolding, hero-metric-card cliches, identical icon+heading+text card grids. Reject anything that reads as templated SaaS-landing-page filler rather than a considered personal site.

## Design Principles
- Show, don't tell — real project outcomes and specifics over adjectives like "passionate" or "innovative"
- Precision as a brand signal — tight typography, deliberate spacing, no filler sections
- One clear path to action — resume, project links, and contact should never be more than a scroll or click away
- Restraint over decoration — every visual choice should earn its place, not scaffold-by-reflex

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA baseline: ≥4.5:1 body text contrast, full keyboard navigation, `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for all motion (Lenis smooth scroll included).
